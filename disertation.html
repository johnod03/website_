Thesis

As a requirement of semester 1 of the iMedia MA/MSc, we were asked to write an initial proposal of what we intend to do as our major project at the end of semester 3. The goal is to identify an area of interest and give an introduction and explaination of this intended project.

This section of the website will contain my initial proposal. As of now, it is still in the gestation period and will need to be developed further with the help and guidance of the faculty at CSIS, University Limerick.

The aim is to get approval for the concept of the proposal first and continue to develop it during semester 2 of the masters course.

Proposal

Initial

The iMedia MA/MSc course, so far, has exposed us, as students, to a broad range of technologies and theories. With a strong focus on interaction design and the creation of multimedia artefacts that address concepts involved with this particular stream.

Idea

My aim is to incorporate many of the the new technologies I have been exposed to during the Masters course and combine them with my prior experience in music, digital interactive installation and art.
